# Simple Salmon Lunch

A quick and easy lunch for salmon lovers

## 1 serving

- 1 salmon fillet
- pepper
- salt
- dill weed
- cayenne pepper

1. preheat the grill to 350F while you prepare the salmon
2. put some olive oil on the salmon
3. season the non-skin side
4. put the salmon on the grill seasoned-side down
5. let it cook on the grill for 3-4 min
6. flip over to skin side and cook for another 3-4 min
7. take off the grill and let sit for a few minutes
8. enjoy!
